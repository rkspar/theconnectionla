About The Connection

Founded by car enthusiast Tony Toutouni, The Connection is an exotic auto customization and repair shop!
We are your number 1 choice, in Los Angeles & Hollywood, if it comes to customizing your luxury or exotic car. Our expert can realize even the most demanding project. Rims / Wheels / Tires / Repair / Body / Wrap / Interior / Performance Upgrades / Audio / Video / GPS / Mobile Internet Solutions, and Electronics are our specialty. Contact us today!

The Connection is your Exotic/Luxury Automobile Specialist. We offer a wide range of services from repair, maintenance, restoration and customizations, to pre-purchase inspections, detailing, paintless ding removal, storage and much more.

We service, Mercedes-Benz, Ferrari, Maserati, BMW, Bentley, Rolls Royce, Jaguar, Lexus, Lamborghini, Porsche, and other foreign and domestic luxury, classic, and exotic cars.
